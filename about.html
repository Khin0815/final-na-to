The people and purpose behind the ELYU Tourism Website.

🌊 Our Mission

ELYU was born from a deep love for La Union — its waves, its culture, its sunsets, and its people. Our mission is to be the most authentic and inspiring digital guide to the Surf Capital of the Northern Philippines, connecting travelers with the best that La Union has to offer.
